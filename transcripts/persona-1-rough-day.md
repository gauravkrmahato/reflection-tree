## A note on this path

The thing I wanted to get right here was *tone*. A person leaning
victim/entitled/self-centric at 9pm after a bad day doesn't need
a lecture about agency. They need someone to sit next to them,
name the feeling, and give them something small for tomorrow.

The closing — "find one small thing you control, and one small
thing you can offer" — is deliberately tiny. Because "take more
responsibility!" is advice that bounces off a tired person. A
two-ingredient experiment is cheap enough to actually try.
